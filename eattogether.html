Let's Eat Together

Have you ever participated in a potluck and wondered what you should bring? Should you bring a finger lickin' good artichoke dip or make your friends loosen their belts a notch with your Salted Caramel Gooey Fudge Squares?

Let's Eat Together helps all the attendees of an event know where the event is, when it is, how many people are coming, and what everyone is bringing. No more guessing only to find out that you're the third person to bring yet another Costco rotisserie chicken!

This app is also fully responsive to tablet and mobile screens.

Technologies used: Node.js, Express, Mongoose, MongoDB, HTML, CSS, jQuery.
