MERCH

MERCH is an e-commerce application for an online purveyor of carefully curated gifts and books. This application allows users to browse products, search for products, add items to a shopping cart, and edit, remove, or checkout items in the shopping cart.

Administrators can log-in to add items to the inventory, update items, delete items, view customer orders, and update order status.

Technologies used: PHP, Codeigniter, MySQL, HTML, CSS, Ajax, Twitter Bootstrap.
